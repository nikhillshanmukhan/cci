INCUBATION CENTER

facilities provided under fab lab

FISAT Fablab will function as an auxiliary body of FISAT Science and Technology Park and Research Centre. A Fabrication Laboratory (FabLab) is a technical prototyping platform for innovation and invention which aims at providing stimulus for students and serves as a platform for learning and innovation.
